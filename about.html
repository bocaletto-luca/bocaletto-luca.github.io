Since childhood, I have lived through the extraordinary technological evolution, immersing myself in the world of computing since 1988. Growing up in an era of rapid innovation, I have explored every advancement in software and hardware, witnessing the legendary battles between Silicon Valley giants who have reshaped the industry year after year. My first computer was a Commodore 64, followed by the Amiga 500 in the 1990s, devices that introduced me to the creative and technical potential of programming and digital development.

My passion for technology has led me to become an expert in the IT sector, with solid experience in software development for PCs, web and web app development, both frontend and backend, using languages such as Python, PHP, JavaScript, and many others. I administer and repair Windows, Linux, and Android systems, which I have followed since their early days, gaining deep knowledge of their architecture and configurations. Over the years, I have repaired and assembled desktop computers, troubleshot issues on laptops, smartphones, and tablets, and worked with 3D printers, modifying them to enhance their functionalities while producing three-dimensional models. I have experimented with programmable devices such as Raspberry Pi and Arduino, exploring the endless possibilities of electronics and automation. Additionally, I have experience in configuring and installing home and office networks, ensuring optimal performance and advanced security.

Beyond my technological career, I have a strong passion for music, playing piano, guitar, keyboards, and synthesizers, and experimenting with music production. I am an independent writer, fascinated by astronomy, radio technology, and robotics, always seeking new knowledge and applications in the field of electronics and innovation.
