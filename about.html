About Me

Bocaletto Luca: Computer Programmer, Artist, Musician, Freelance Writer. Projects published freely on GitHub in various programming languages including Python, SQL, PHP, HTML, CSS, and more.

Something of Me

I have been passionate about computing for over 30 years. I enjoy managing both Windows and GNU/Linux operating systems since my earliest experiences. My goal is that my experience helps you overcome everyday tech challenges, boosting productivity in work, study, or entertainment.

My hobbies and expertise include: Freelance writing, technology consulting, full stack web and software development, IT support, making, 3D printing and design. I experiment with and promote electronics, robotics, and home automation with Arduino. My journey began with the C64 and cassette copies—my very first hack as a child—and evolved through video game development engines. I am also passionate about astronomy, radio, chess, and music (piano, keyboards, synthesizers, organ, guitar, bass—plus an enthusiasm to "Pump High the Volume!").
