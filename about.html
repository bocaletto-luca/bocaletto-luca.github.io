Something of Me

My hobbies and expertise include freelance writing, technology consulting, full stack web and software development, IT support, making, 3D printing and design, as well as experimenting with electronics, robotics, and home automation with Arduino. I began with the C64 and cassette copies – my very first hack – and later explored video game development. I’m also passionate about astronomy, radio, chess, and music.
